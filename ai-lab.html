🤖 AI Lab

Dùng AI để học tiếng Anh đúng cách

Trang này không giả vờ thay giáo viên. Nó giúp bạn dùng AI như tutor, coach, examiner và planner bằng prompt rõ ràng.

Principles

AI nên làm gì cho việc học của bạn?

AI mạnh nhất khi dùng để tạo feedback, gợi ý, biến đổi input và ép bạn sản xuất output — không phải để làm bài hộ.

Tutor

Giải thích grammar, vocab, pronunciation theo cách bạn hiểu.

Coach

Lập study plan, nhắc việc và theo dõi lỗi lặp lại.

Examiner

Chấm speaking/writing theo tiêu chí rõ ràng.

Prompt library

Prompt mẫu để copy

Bấm copy để dùng với trợ lý AI bạn chọn.

Vocab coach

Act as my English vocabulary coach at B1-B2 level. Teach me 10 useful collocations about work and study. For each item, give meaning, pronunciation tips, one natural example, one short exercise, and ask me to create my own sentence.

Grammar explainer

Act as my grammar tutor. Explain the difference between present perfect and past simple in Vietnamese-friendly terms, then give me 8 contrastive examples, 5 mini questions, and correct my answers one by one.

Speaking examiner

Act as a B2 speaking examiner. Give me one speaking prompt, let me answer, then assess my response for fluency, grammar, vocabulary and coherence. Highlight 5 specific improvements and rewrite my answer in a stronger B2 version.

Writing reviewer

Act as my B2 writing coach. I will paste my paragraph. First, rate task response, organisation, grammar and vocabulary from 1 to 5. Then show my repeated mistakes, suggest stronger alternatives, and give me a revised version that still sounds like a learner, not a native speaker.

Listening coach

Act as my listening coach. Create a short transcript about travel at B1-B2 level, then remove 10 keywords to make a gap-fill dictation. After I answer, check it and explain which listening clues I missed.

Study planner

Act as my English study planner. My goal is B2 in 12 weeks. I can study 60 minutes a day. Make me a weekly plan with vocabulary, grammar, listening, reading, speaking and writing. Add review days and tell me how to measure progress every Sunday.
